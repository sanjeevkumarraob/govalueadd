What we do

Value Add Realty provides a full range of services to local and NRI investors, buyers, and real estate lenders.

A team of experienced professionals is dedicated to enhancing the value of client investments throughout Bangalore, Karnataka.

Value Add Realty’s primary business lines are real estate and brokerage, real estate closing and advisory services, business broking, extensive retail brand development through franchise consulting and advisory services.

In the field of real estate management, Value Add Realty is involved throughout the entire segment of a property i.e. residential and commercial buying/selling/leasing. A team is dedicated to enhancing the relationship of client’s interests throughout Bangalore, Karnataka.

In the business broking segment, it is associated with the Asias largest franchise solutions company ‘Franchise India Brands Ltd’ as a certified business associate and extends full fledged assistance in the franchise industry to its clients.

Value Add Realty also offers an array of pre and post closing services including due diligence, title consolidation, payment of property taxes, property inspections and financing arrangement and referrals, and sales documentation.
